Bharat Socials

Bharat Socials serves as a bridge between NGOs and college students from Placeholder University. It allows volunteers to engage in various channels related to social causes like education, women's empowerment, health, and environmental issues. NGOs can post their events, and volunteers can easily track their attendance and participation.
