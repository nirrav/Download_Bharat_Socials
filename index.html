Bharat Socials

Bharat Socials is a platform where government-registered NGOs collaborate with Placeholder University and connect to the NSS (National Service Scheme) of various colleges. Volunteers can join broadcast channels based on domains such as Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan, Women Empowerment, Health & Hygiene, and Environmental Conservation. NGOs can post events, and volunteers can bookmark them. NGOs can track volunteer attendance.
